Elevator Pitch

RedFlag is a browser extension designed to reduce the spread of online misinformation. It runs in the background while users browse social media and news sites and automatically highlights potentially false or misleading content directly on the screen. Instead of relying on users to fact-check information themselves, RedFlag brings awareness to misinformation at the moment it’s encountered. With one click, users can view a short explanation and links to credible sources, helping them make informed decisions before believing or sharing content.
